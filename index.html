about Destin

Destin AI is born out of Founders Institute in May 2017. Besides being an Artificially Intelligent bot Destin is also Emotionally Intelligent. Its purpose is to create a pleasant experience for people on their new journey to Canada. Destin Bot and our professional immigration lawyers will make sure that you receive correct and accurate answers to your immigration related questions, prepare your documents through the help of Destin Bot and connect with the right immigration lawyers based on your preferences.

We are getting prepared for the launch of Destin Bot for 1 November 2017.In the meantime, we can manually serve you. If you have any questions regarding Canadian Immigration feel free to send us an email or simply subscribe to our e-mail list so that we can exclusively alert you when our bot is ready.
